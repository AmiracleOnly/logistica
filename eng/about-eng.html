OUR SERVICES

WHO WE ARE

Forward Logistic Group is a logistics company based in Kazakhstan, which provides full range of services in the field of cargo transportation, warehouse storage and customs registration. The company offers its logistics services both within Kazakhstan and internationally. We guarantee:

HIGH SPEED

We shall promptly complete all stages of the logistics process: from reception of order to cargo delivery

SAFETY

We shall ensure safety of cargo at all stages of transportation and storage

CONTROL

We control and manage logistics processes at all stages of service provision

COMPANY AIMS

To provide reliable, fast and efficient logistics solutions that will help clients optimize their business processes.

MISSION OF THE COMPANY

To become a link between manufacturers and consumers by way of providing safe, prompt and economically efficient delivery of cargo.
